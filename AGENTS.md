**claril** — Open-source AI-powered architecture & process modeling workbench (BPMN, Sequence, C4) with a deterministic logic inspector and AI advisor.

**Goal:** Claril is an open-source, self-hostable architecture & process intelligence workbench for Solution Architects and Architects. It unifies BPMN process models, UML sequence diagrams, and C4 architecture models in one tool, backed by a deterministic logic inspector that catches structural defects and an AI advisor (brand-agnostic, BYOK) that critiques design — grounded by an organization-level Asset Catalog so services are defined once and understood everywhere. The tool is fully useful with zero AI configured; AI is an amplifier, never a gate.
