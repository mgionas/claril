# Roadmap

Sequencing principle: **prove the moat (deterministic inspector on a beautiful canvas) before the expensive collaboration plumbing.** Each phase is independently shippable.

## Phases (north star)

- **P0 — Foundation** · monorepo, Next.js 16 app shell, themed bpmn-js canvas, BPMN import/export, Postgres (Drizzle), Better Auth (org→workspace→project), docker-compose. **✅ shipped**
- **P1 — Logic Inspector ★** · graph model + structural/best-practice rules, findings panel (fly-to + quick-fixes), CLI/MCP lint mode. **✅ shipped**
- **P2 — AI Advisor + Asset Catalog** · provider-agnostic BYOK `LLMProvider`, advisor grounded on findings, doc-gen, built-in asset types, 3-tier UX. **✅ shipped** — since matured into a full AI **co-editor** (see W9–W11).
- **P3 — Versioning & multi-diagram** · named/auto versions, visual diff, restore; Sequence + C4 editors; cross-diagram asset binding. **✅ shipped** (versioning via W10/F1; Sequence + C4 are Mermaid-based).
- **P4 — Team & collaboration** · RBAC polish, comments/@mentions, review workflow, real-time multiplayer (Yjs + Hocuspocus), SSO/SCIM. **◧ in progress** — W16 comments & @mentions + notification bell shipped (first slice); W17 review workflow + W18 multiplayer ahead.
- **P5 — Platform & ecosystem** · full user-defined Asset Catalog + impact analysis, DMN, simulation, executable export (Zeebe/Flowable), plugin SDK, integrations. **◻ not started**

## Where we are (2026-06-25)

P0–P3 are **built and deployed to production on Vercel** (`apps/web`, Neon Postgres, migrations through **0013**). The product today: self-hostable, authed, multi-tenant; draw/save/version BPMN (+ Sequence/C4) on a polished shadcn-sidebar shell with **light & dark mode** (system-aware, full canvas); a deterministic inspector (also CLI/MCP); an Asset Catalog; an AI **co-editor** (chats, documents, reviews, edits — BYOK across 6 providers; chat is **private per user**); **Personal & Organization tenancy** with Workspaces + roles; **team collaboration** (comments, @mentions, notification bell, per-version authors); and diagram **export** (.bpmn / PNG / PDF — PNG/PDF in light or dark).

The repo is **public** (AGPL-3.0, github.com/mgionas/claril) with branch protection + Dependabot.

The recent push turned the AI from advisor → **co-editor**: tabbed Chat/Problems drawer (W9), History + AI-edit review + persistent chat (W10), a broad BPMN editing op-set (W11), and multi-provider BYOK + model switcher (W8). The AI editing **works end-to-end on all providers** but its **plan quality is not yet fully trusted** — see Known issues. On top of the editor: a UX revamp (W12), shadcn sidebar shell (W14) + management surfaces (W15), Personal/Org tenancy with Workspaces (W13), and collaboration P1 — comments & @mentions (W16). Latest (2026-06-25): **light & dark mode** (next-themes, system-aware, full canvas incl. minimap; toggle in top bar + Settings; marketing dark-only); a **theme-aware export modal** (PNG/PDF in light or dark); **per-version authors** in History; a **multi-pool lane-recognition fix** (geometry no longer steals nodes across pools; sub-lanes keep their pool); **true multi-pool generation** (W11 P5.3); and a batch of canvas polish — dark-themed bpmn-js chrome (replace popup, rename box, error tooltip, no white drag-flash, no focus ring), pool-grows-to-fit on AI edits, process-level findings now visible on the board, and markers no longer vanish when deleting a flagged element. Earlier: diagram export, comments on personal diagrams + right-click "Comment", auto-set AI default on connect, crash-safe key decryption, a global navigation progress bar, and personal-scope hardening.

## Known issues (open)

- **AI editing plan quality** — the over-engineering drift (inventing pools / message flows / deleting tasks for a simple "notify"/"move") is now guarded deterministically: **scope guard** (rejects pool/lane/message-flow/node-delete ops the instruction didn't authorize) + **soundness validation** (simulates the plan and rejects results with new structural errors), both folded into the planner's self-repair retry (W11 Phase 5.1–5.2). Pool-safe layout (Phase 5.3) is now **shipped** — multi-pool generation lays out + draws message flows correctly. *Pending live confirmation the scope/soundness guards are enough for the over-engineering drift.*
- **Live smoke tests pending** for W10 (F1/F2/F3) and W11 (P1–P3) — exercised informally during dev; formal pass + sign-off still outstanding.

*Resolved since last update: AI chat is now **private per user** (`chat_message` scoped to `(diagramId, userId)`, migration 0013); multi-pool **lane mis-recognition** fixed (Theming & polish 2); process-level findings now show on the board.*
